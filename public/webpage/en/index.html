Website "ActiveBeach.in.th" is a result from the research entitled " An Innovation Development of Intelligent Recommendations System for Supporting Wellness Tourism in Active Beach Provinces (Chonburi, Rayong, Chanthaburi, Trad) For High Value Services Construction". The research fund was supported by the national budget of Suan Dusit Univesity, Bangkok, Thailand through the year 2019.
